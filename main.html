HERBAL PLANTS AND ITS USE


Aloe Vera or Aloe barbadensis is a plant with a short stem that stores water in its leaves. It is also known as ‘Ghritkumari’ in Hindi. The gel present in the leaves can be consumed by making juice out of them. Aloe Vera contains vitamin C, vitamin E, vitamin B9, and vitamin B12.

Curry leaves are aromatic leaves that come from the curry tree. They're commonly used in Indian cooking for their unique flavor. These leaves add a citrusy and slightly bitter taste to dishes like soups, curries, and chutneys.

Rosemary is a fragrant evergreen herb native to the Mediterranean. It is used as a culinary condiment, an ingredient in perfumes, and for its potential health benefits. Like oregano, thyme, basil, and lavender, rosemary is a member of the mint family Lamiaceae.
